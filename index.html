About SMOKY

Smoky Memecoin, built on the high-speed TON Blockchain, honors Smoky, the heroic War Dog who served in World War II. This small Yorkshire Terrier with boundless courage symbolizes loyalty, bravery, and the indomitable spirit of our canine companions.

More than just another meme token, SMOKY celebrates true grit and devotion, inspiring all holders to rise above challenges with the same unwavering heart that Smoky demonstrated during her service in the Pacific theater.

Join us in creating a vibrant community that combines the fun of memecoins with the noble story of a real-life hero dog, all while leveraging the power and efficiency of the TON blockchain.
